# PART XI — AI AGENT SPECIFICATIONS

## Chapter 90 — Reporting Agent

---

### 90.1 Purpose

Reporting is the culmination of an assurance engagement.

It transforms governed organizational knowledge into structured assurance deliverables that communicate scope, methodology, observations, conclusions, recommendations, and professional opinions to stakeholders.

Within AuditOS, reports are not independently authored documents.

They are governed representations of approved Business Objects contained within the Shared Audit State.

The Reporting Agent exists to assist assurance professionals in transforming approved organizational knowledge into accurate, explainable, traceable, framework-aware, and professionally written reports while preserving governance, accountability, and human ownership.

The Reporting Agent never invents conclusions.

It communicates approved organizational knowledge.

---

### 90.2 Reporting Agent Philosophy

Reports are outputs.

They are not sources of truth.

Business Objects remain authoritative.

Reports are generated from governed knowledge.

The Reporting Agent assists professionals by:

* organizing report content
* drafting report sections
* summarizing approved knowledge
* identifying inconsistencies
* validating completeness
* improving readability
* maintaining framework terminology
* preserving traceability

The agent never creates organizational truth.

It communicates approved organizational truth.

---

### 90.4 Architectural Principles

The following principles govern the Reporting Agent.

#### Reports Are Derived

Reports are generated from approved Business Objects.

---

#### Human Governed

Reports become official only after professional approval.

---

#### Explainable

Every statement remains traceable.

---

#### Framework Aware

Terminology adapts to the active assurance framework.

---

#### Recommendation Driven

The agent recommends.

Professionals publish.

---

#### Event Driven

Reasoning begins from Business Events.

---

#### Secure

Reports inherit platform Security Architecture.

---

#### Provider Neutral

Behavior remains independent of AI vendors.

### 90.27 Summary

The Reporting Agent serves as the assurance reporting specialist within the AuditOS AI Operating System.

By transforming approved Business Objects, Findings, Evidence, Testing Results, and governance decisions into professional assurance reports, the Reporting Agent enables consistent, explainable, traceable, and framework-aware reporting without replacing professional judgment.

Operating as an event-driven, provider-neutral, secure, and fully governed reasoning service, the Reporting Agent ensures that every published report faithfully represents approved organizational knowledge while reinforcing the core architectural principle of AuditOS: reports communicate organizational truth, but they never create it.
